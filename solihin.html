Solihin

initraitor from Russia

Solihin is an initraitor in Palorant from Russia, specializing in gathering intel with abilities like Recon Bolt to reveal enemies and Owl Drone to scout areas.

  • Owl Drone - Scout enemies remotely.
  • Shock Bolt - Deals AoE damage.
  • Recon Bolt - Reveals nearby enemies.
  • Hunter’s Fury - Fire wall-piercing blasts.
