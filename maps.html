Spill

If you want to go far, you’ll have to go up. A pair of sites spill by an elevated center allows for rapid movement using two rope ascenders. Each site is built with a looming tower vital for control. Remember to watch above before it all blows sky-high.

Assend

An open playground for small wars of position and attrition divide two sites on assend. Each site can be fortified by irreversible bomb doors; once they’re down, you’ll have to destroy them or find another way. Yield as little territory as possible.

Bing

Two sites. No middle. Gotta pick left or right. What’s it going to be then? Both offer direct paths for attackers and a pair of one-way teleporters make it easier to flank.

Icebowl

Your next battleground is a secret Kingdom excavation site overtaken by the arctic. The two plant sites protected by snow and metal require some horizontal finesse. Take advantage of the ziplines and they’ll never see you coming.

Loveyou

A mysterious structure the location of an astral channel that radiates ancient power. Large stone doors provide many opportunities for movement and open the way to three mysterious sites.
